Visitor Services

Importance of Visiting Patients

Visiting loved ones in the hospital is not just a gesture of care. It plays a crucial role in their recovery journey.
The emotional support and connection provided by family and friends can significantly uplift
the spirits of patients.

Cards & Flowers

Sending a patient a card, flowers or a gift can often make them feel better and make their time in the hospital
seem to go by faster. The Hospital gift shop takes phone orders.
To place an order, please call 012-345-6789.
To find out where a patient is located, please call 012-345-6789. Please be sure to include the patient’s name and room number.

WiFi Use

Free, public wireless Internet access is available in all all floors of the hospital facilities. Through this network, you can use your laptop or mobile device to access the Internet from patients hospital rooms, waiting areas, cafeterias and other locations.

Accessing WiFi :
  • The wireless network is called "H-indicator-OOHS."
  • Start up your laptop or mobile device, and enter the address of any Web site.
  • You'll be redirected to a login page.
  • Enter your last name and e-mail address, and click “Accept.”
  • On the next page, click "Continue".
Access Note :
  • WiFi connections made through the "guest" network are not secure.
  • The faculty and staff may not use the network to access secure areas of the health system's Web site.
  • The free WiFi service does not provide access to certain inappropriate Web sites, using the Web filtering software that meets industry standards.
  • The hospital does not provide technical support for this service, and is not responsible for computers and devices that are lost, damaged or stolen.
Note to Patients:

Please do not use the network while you are in an outpatient exam room, or when a member of your health care team visits your inpatient room.

Food

Enjoy a variety of fresh and nourishing meal options at our cafeteria. We strive to provide a comforting dining
experience for visitors. Discover a diverse array of culinary delights at our hospital and the surrounding area, offering an exquisite
selection of food, snacks, and coffee options for your comfort and enjoyment.
For a comprehensive list of dining options within the hospital premises and local vicinity, please refer to our
dedicated culinary guide available in the patient rooms.

Ordering:
  • To place an order, simply dial the provided extension in your patient room. Our dedicated staff will assist you with menu choices, dietary preferences, and any special request.

Overnight-Stay

Our overnight accommodations are designed with your comfort in mind, providing a home-like environment that allows you to be in close proximity to your hospitalized family members. Here's what you can expect from our
overnight stay options:

  1. Convenient Location: Located within the hospital vicinity, our accommodations are strategically placed to
    minimize travel time, ensuring you can swiftly reach your loved one's side whenever needed.
  2. Secure Access: Your safety and security are our top priorities. Rest assured that our accommodations have
    secure access measures in place, providing you with peace of mind during your stay.
  3. Booking and Inquiries: To inquire about availability or make a reservation, please contact our dedicated
    accommodations desk at 012-345-6789. Our friendly staff will assist you with the booking process and
    address any questions you may have.

During challenging times, being close to your loved ones can be a source of strength and solace. Our overnight
accommodations aim to provide a supportive space for you to rest and recharge, ensuring you can be there for
your family members throughout their healing journey. Your comfort is our priority.
